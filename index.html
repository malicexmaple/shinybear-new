Sickyaki 生病小鸡

Meet Sickyaki, the adorable yellow chick who loves to dance and share special moments with her beloved companion Pingdori! She's a small, weak chick that is crinkly, doesn't have energy to take problems seriously, and lives in a desolate city, adapting in her own way. 认识生病小鸡，这只可爱的黄色小鸡喜欢跳舞，并与她心爱的伙伴企鹅朋友分享特殊时刻！她是一只小小的、虚弱的小鸡，皱巴巴的，没有精力认真对待问题，住在一个荒凉的城市里，以自己的方式适应着。

Pingdori 企鹅朋友

Say hello to Pingdori, the cutest gray penguin who shares an unbreakable bond with Sickyaki and loves expressing affection! He's a round gray penguin who cares for Sickyaki, gentle and nurturing companion, often shown massaging or comforting Yaki. 向企鹅朋友问好，这只最可爱的灰色企鹅与生病小鸡有着不可分割的纽带，喜欢表达爱意！他是一只圆圆的灰色企鹅，照顾着生病小鸡，是温柔且有nurturing的伙伴，经常被描绘为给小鸡按摩或安慰。

Together Forever 永远在一起

Sickyaki and Pingdori represent the beautiful bond of friendship and care. Created by Korean artist Wookiema (@sick_yaki, 1M+ followers), these characters have touched hearts worldwide with their gentle, vulnerable, and endearing personalities. 生病小鸡和企鹅朋友代表着友谊和关怀的美好纽带。由韩国艺术家Wookiema创作（@sick_yaki，100万+粉丝），这些角色以其温柔、脆弱和可爱的个性触动了全世界的心。
